About

Meet Sandy.

Sandy Bains is a REALTOR® with HomeLife/Silvercity Realty Inc., Brokerage, serving Brampton and the Greater Toronto Area. Her practice is built on a simple idea: treat every purchase and sale as if it were her own.

That means honest advice even when it's inconvenient, pricing grounded in real comparables rather than wishful thinking, and steady communication so you're never left wondering what happens next.

Whether you're a first-time buyer trying to make sense of the market, a growing family trading up, or a homeowner ready to downsize, Sandy brings the same patience and attention to every file — because behind every transaction is a family making one of the biggest decisions of their lives.

  • Neighbourhood shortlists & private showings
  • Offer strategy and negotiation

Selling

Priced right, presented well

  • Comparative market analysis
  • Staging and preparation advice
  • MLS® listing with professional marketing
  • Negotiation through to closing

Leasing & investing

How it works

A clear process, so you always know what's next.

01

We talk

A no-obligation conversation about your goals, budget and timing — by phone, at the office or over coffee.

02

We plan

A pricing strategy for sellers, or a financing-ready shortlist for buyers. Everything in writing, nothing vague.

03

We act

Showings, listings, offers and counter-offers — handled promptly, with updates at every step.

We close

Inspections, lawyers, paperwork and keys. Sandy stays on the file until you're settled, not just until it's signed.
